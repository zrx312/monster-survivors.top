About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you face endless waves of monsters. Collect power-ups, upgrade your character, and survive as long as possible in this challenging adventure.

The game features procedurally generated levels, ensuring every playthrough offers a unique experience. With various character classes to choose from and dozens of upgrades to discover, you'll find endless replayability as you strive to beat your high score.

How to Play

Monster Survivors is easy to learn but difficult to master. Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character around the map. Your character will automatically attack nearby enemies. Collect experience gems to level up and choose new upgrades.

The key to success is positioning and prioritizing the right upgrades for your character class. Try to keep monsters at a distance while maximizing your damage output.

Tips and Strategies

  • Focus on movement speed upgrades early to help avoid monsters
  • Don't ignore defensive upgrades like armor and health regeneration
  • Try to maintain distance from large groups of enemies
  • Prioritize eliminating special monsters with unique abilities
  • Experiment with different character classes to find your preferred playstyle
